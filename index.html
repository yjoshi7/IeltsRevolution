IELTS Band Scores Explained

Band Score Skill Level Description
9 Expert Full operational command of the language
8 Very Good Fully operational command with occasional inaccuracies
7 Good Operational command though with occasional inaccuracies
6 Competent Effective command despite some inaccuracies
5 Modest Partial command of the language, coping with overall meaning
4 Limited Basic competence limited to familiar situations

Academic vs General Training

Academic IELTS

For students applying to universities or professional registration in English-speaking countries.

  • More complex reading texts
  • Academic writing tasks
  • Same listening/speaking as General

General Training

For migration to English-speaking countries or work/training programs.

  • Everyday reading materials
  • Letter writing tasks
  • Same listening/speaking as Academic
